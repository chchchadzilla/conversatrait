Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Convers-a-trait.

How accurate are the personality assessments?

Our assessments are based on validated psychological frameworks like MBTI and Big Five, but go beyond traditional questionnaires by analyzing your actual communication patterns. This provides a more authentic picture of your personality than self-reported surveys, with accuracy rates of 85-92% compared to professional assessments.

How secure is my data?

We take security seriously. Your data is encrypted both in transit and at rest. We maintain strict access controls and never share your personal information with third parties. You can delete your data at any time. Our detailed privacy policy outlines how we use (and don't use) your information.

Can I use this for job candidate screening?

Yes, our Enterprise tier offers powerful candidate comparison tools to help you identify the best fits for your team. However, we recommend using our insights as one component of a comprehensive hiring process, not as the sole decision factor. Our system can help identify communication strengths and potential areas for development.

How much text do you need for an accurate analysis?

For basic personality insights, we need at least 500 words of natural communication. For more comprehensive analyses, including hidden talents and relationship dynamics, we recommend at least 2,000 words. The more text you provide, the more accurate and detailed our analysis will be.

Can I analyze someone else's communications?

You can analyze publicly available communications like public social media posts, published articles, or historical texts. However, for ethical and legal reasons, we don't allow analysis of private communications from other individuals without their explicit consent. All users are required to agree to our terms of service, which prohibit unauthorized analysis of others' private communications.

When will Convers-a-trait be available?

We're launching our beta version in Q3 2025, with waitlist members getting first access. Full public release is planned for Q4 2025. Join our waitlist to stay updated on our progress and be among the first to experience Convers-a-trait.
